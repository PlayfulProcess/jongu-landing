AI Governance Framework

The theoretical foundation behind our experimental governance system. The v0.1 prototype above demonstrates these principles in action.

Our Governance Philosophy

We transform the collective by transforming ourselves. Our AI governance system evaluates decisions based on Recursive.eco's core principles rather than pure democracy. Community input guides the AI, but decisions align with our foundational values of individual transformation serving collective evolution.

The Beauty of Recursive Governance

Elegant simplicity: Humans and AI recursively working together to optimize governance. This isn't complex bureaucracy‚Äîit's adaptive intelligence. The v0.1 system above demonstrates how beautifully simple this can be when designed for actual human needs rather than theoretical perfection.

Decisions get made by humans interacting with and customizing the Claude artifact themselves. Anyone can change the AI's prompt, synthesize different principles, and share their conversations with the AI that led to better decisions. Whoever's artifact produces the last decision becomes the tool that guides the collective‚Äîuntil someone invents something better.

How AI Governance Works

1

Community Proposes

Anyone can propose new rules, report disputes, or suggest system changes. Community voting provides valuable input and sentiment.

2

AI Evaluates

Our AI system evaluates each proposal against the five core principles, providing detailed reasoning and scoring for transparency.

3

Decision & Implementation

Decisions are implemented based on principle alignment. Community can appeal, and founders maintain override capability for vision protection.

4

Recursive Improvement

The community can propose changes to the governance system itself, allowing for continuous evolution while maintaining core principles.
