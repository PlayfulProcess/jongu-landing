Key Questions & Enablers

These aren't just features‚Äîthey're attempts to create the psychological conditions where authentic community can emerge. Donation-Sustained Access

Key Question: Could a platform be sustained entirely through voluntary donations, ensuring people can access resources based on need rather than economic ability?

Why This Matters: When access isn't tied to purchasing power, people can focus on genuine contribution rather than financial transactions. A $10 tool feels very different to someone in Brazil versus Silicon Valley.

Privacy by Design

Principle: People can navigate and use all tools without any data collection. No tracking, no profiles, no surveillance.

Why This Matters: When people aren't being monitored and monetized, they can be authentic. True connection requires psychological safety from surveillance.

‚úÖ Implementation: No cookies, no tracking scripts. Ability to use tools without data sharing (just refrain from logging in)
‚ú®

Optional AI Enhancement

Principle: AI assistance is always optional. All self-hosted tools provide full value without requiring AI interaction.

Why This Matters: People should have agency over their relationship with AI. Some want assistance, others prefer pure human-to-human interaction.

‚úÖ Current: AI chat is opt-in feature
üåä

Calm Platform + Deep Connection Spaces

Approach: Jongu itself remains calm and accessible. For those wanting deeper connection, Discord channels offer collaboration, notifications, and habit-building support.

Why This Matters: Different people need different levels of engagement. Some want quiet reflection, others want active community involvement.
